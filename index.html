Focaccia whit Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped eith sweet caramelized onions,tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons istant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

    For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegard
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
